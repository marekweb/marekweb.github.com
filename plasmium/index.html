Plasmium

The evil Vectroids have invaded space. Can you save Earth from destruction?

Plasmium challenges you to fight the alien onslaught in a non-stop action battle. Earth is under siege and you're the last defence.

  • Plasma-powered weapons: homing missiles, artillery shells, plasma snakes and more.
  • Fast paced gameplay: catch combos and frenzies for crazy bonuses.
  • Vast alien fleet: fight the Vectroid armada including Shieldoids, Repulsotrons and Carriers.
  • Interactive environments: take advantage of your surroundings by smashing asteroids into enemies in the Asteroid Belt and triggering electrical discharges in the Nebula.
  • Achievements: have your feats immortalized and be the talk of the galaxy.
  • Epic mothership battles: blow up the alien mothership and save humanity.
